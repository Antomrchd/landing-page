Why Paris

Beyond the postcard.

Paris is not just a destination — it is a state of mind. Beyond the iconic landmarks lies a city of extraordinary refinement: private ateliers, hidden courtyards, Michelin-starred bistros tucked into unmarked doorways. With Mythios Travels, you experience the Paris that most visitors never see.

From a penthouse suite overlooking the Seine to a private evening at the Louvre, we open doors that remain closed to the ordinary traveller.
