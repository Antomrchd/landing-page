Why Cannes

Where glamour meets the sea.

Cannes is the French Riviera at its most glamorous. La Croisette, the legendary boulevard lined with palm trees and palace hotels, sets the tone for a destination that has attracted the world's elite for over a century. Film stars, royalty and the ultra-wealthy converge here each year — and for good reason.

Beyond the Festival, Cannes offers private beach clubs, Michelin-starred restaurants and some of the most spectacular yacht charters on the Mediterranean.

Best season May – September
Language French (English widely spoken)
Currency Euro (€)
Hotel standard Palace & 5-star only
Ideal duration 3 – 6 nights
