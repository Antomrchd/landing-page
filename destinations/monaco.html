Why Monaco

Where luxury is a way of life.

Monaco is the world's most concentrated expression of wealth, glamour and ambition. In just two square kilometres, the Principality packs more superyachts, Michelin stars and designer boutiques than most countries manage in their entirety. It is not just a destination — it is an experience of the extraordinary.

With Mythios Travels, you gain access to the Casino de Monte-Carlo's private rooms, front-row tickets to the Formula 1 Grand Prix and suites in the most exclusive hotels in the world.

Best season April – October (Grand Prix in May)
Language French (English widely spoken)
Currency Euro (€)
Hotel standard Palace & ultra-luxury only
Ideal duration 2 – 5 nights
