Why Saint Barthélemy

The Caribbean, elevated.

Saint Barthélemy — or St. Barts — is the Caribbean at its most sophisticated. A small French island with no mass tourism, no casinos and no chain hotels. What it does have is extraordinary: pristine white-sand beaches, world-class restaurants, a deeply French joie de vivre and a clientele that includes some of the world's most discerning travellers.

With Mythios Travels, you access hillside villas with private infinity pools, exclusive beach clubs and the finest yacht charters in the Caribbean.

Best season December – April
Language French (English widely spoken)
Currency Euro (€)
Hotel standard Luxury villas & boutique hotels
Ideal duration 7 – 10 nights
