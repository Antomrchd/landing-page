Why Amalfi Coast

Italy at its most breathtaking.

The Amalfi Coast is a UNESCO World Heritage Site — 50 kilometres of sheer cliffs plunging into the Mediterranean, draped in lemon groves, bougainvillea and pastel-coloured villages that cling impossibly to the rock. It is one of the world's most dramatically beautiful coastlines, and one of its most exclusive.

With Mythios Travels, you explore it from the water aboard a private yacht, dine in Positano's finest restaurants and stay in clifftop villas with pools that seem to float above the sea.

Best season May – June · September – October
