Why Seychelles

The last paradise on earth.

The Seychelles archipelago is one of the world's most pristine destinations — 115 islands scattered across the Indian Ocean, home to some of the most extraordinary marine life and landscapes on the planet. Here, luxury means privacy, space and a connection to nature that is becoming increasingly rare.

With Mythios Travels, you access private island resorts, overwater villas and exclusive diving experiences in some of the world's most protected coral reefs.

Best season April – May · October – November
Language Creole, French, English
Currency Seychellois Rupee (SCR)
Hotel standard Private islands & overwater villas
Ideal duration 8 – 14 nights
