Why Miami

Where energy meets elegance.

Miami is unlike any other American city. A place where Art Deco meets ultra-modern architecture, where world-class art galleries sit beside pristine beaches, and where the energy of the night is matched only by the brilliance of the day. Miami's luxury scene has evolved dramatically — it is now one of the world's most exciting destinations for the discerning traveller.

With Mythios Travels, you stay in the finest penthouse suites, access private art collections during Art Basel and explore Miami's culinary scene with a personal chef and sommelier.

Best season November – April (dry season)
