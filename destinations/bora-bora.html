Why Bora Bora

A lagoon like no other.

Bora Bora is, quite simply, one of the most beautiful places on earth. Surrounded by a lagoon of impossible turquoise and protected by a barrier reef, the island rises dramatically from the sea in a single volcanic peak. It is the quintessential overwater bungalow destination — and also so much more.

With Mythios Travels, you stay in the finest overwater villas, dive the lagoon at dawn and dine on the beach as the sun sets behind Mount Otemanu.

Best season May – October (dry season)
Language French, Tahitian (English spoken)
Currency CFP Franc (XPF)
Hotel standard Overwater villas & private resorts
Ideal duration 7 – 12 nights
