Why Bali

Luxury in perfect harmony.

Bali is unlike anywhere else on earth. An island of ancient temples, terraced rice fields and a culture built on beauty and ritual — yet also home to some of the world's most extraordinary private villas and wellness retreats. Bali does luxury differently: it is intimate, serene and deeply human.

With Mythios Travels, you stay in cliff-edge villas with private infinity pools, access traditional healing ceremonies and dine under the stars in the heart of the jungle.

Best season April – October (dry season)
Language Balinese / Indonesian (English spoken)
Currency Indonesian Rupiah (IDR)
Hotel standard Private villas & 5-star resorts
Ideal duration 7 – 14 nights
